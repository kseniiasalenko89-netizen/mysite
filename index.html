About Me

My path to healing

My journey began with my own search for balance — burnout, back pain, a body that felt disconnected from who I was inside. Traditional Thai massage was the first thing that truly helped. I went to Chiang Mai to learn it properly, and never looked back.

Today I combine three disciplines that work together beautifully: the precise bodywork of Thai massage, the breath and movement of yoga, and the constitutional wisdom of Ayurveda. Every session I offer is shaped around one person — you.
